Budgeting

A plugin called BimBon was used to automatically generate the building cost and material specification, so that with each modification of the original design the estimate cost is recalculated. Woka explores the integration between Wikihouse, Sketchup and Bimbon as an interface that mediates the design and building process of the house. It is not a finished house, but an open construction system based on parametric design and digital fabrication intended to trigger the design and building process .
