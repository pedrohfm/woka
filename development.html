Early Stages

During the early stages of this research we focused primarily on creating a model, so as to study the joints system and their organization. We intend to follow up with a study on how different materials can interact and be coupled with the structure, most importantly the ones that people have at hand more frequently, such as plywood sheets, wood sticks or bricks for example.
